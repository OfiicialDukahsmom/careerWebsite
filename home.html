Software Engineer

Software Engineering is a spectacular fit for my interests, this career I have researched is not just about the salary, its also about your personality, your cooperation, and your dedication. No wise person would pursue a job just for the salary, they'd want to look for a job that's interesting to them, something they have a passion for, something that fits right for them. And for me, Software Engineering is exactly that, no place have I ever been that has made as comfortable as programming has, I pass six hours like it's one just programming, I have delicate care for my work and put my best work into them, making them serve as benchmarks for my skill. I upload my personal projects on github as well as Computer Science assignments, overtime it'll show me how far I've come as a programmer, including this project here.



A good Software Engineer..


  • Is determined
  • Actively learns and adapts as tech advances
  • Is a lovely team player and has powerful coordination with their team
  • Always up for challenges if it means sharpening their axe of skill
  • Consistently investigates code and has a desire to learn what is happpening in the code



Disorders that can actually help you with Software Engineering



  1. Aspergers (I have this!)
  2. From hyperfocus and viewing things differently, autism can actually benefit your Software Engineering
  3. OCD (It feels like I have this)
  4. Wanting to perfect your work is either a strength or a weakness, but it does help you make websites as best as possible, just don't spend too much time on it
