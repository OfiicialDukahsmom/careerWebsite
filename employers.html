Picking the Right Employer


If You Want Research & AI

  1. Prioritize OpenAI and Google for deep ML work.
  2. Build strong math and research experience alongside production code.

If You Love Products & UX

  1. Look at Apple, Meta, and Netflix for user-facing impact.
  2. Focus on design, polish, and end-to-end ownership of features.
