Salary & Benefits for a Software Engineer


Software engineering compensation is more than just a single number. Base salary, stock, bonuses, and benefits all combine to create a complete picture of how well a role supports your life and long-term goals. This page highlights typical pay at several major tech companies and showcases the benefits that make each opportunity unique.



Sample Software Engineer Salaries

Company Average Base Salary + Average Stock (/yr) + Average Bonus = Average Total Compensation Source(s)

Benefits



Apple Benefits

Free snacks and drinks, gym discounts, on-site wellness clinic, employer-funded HSA, fertility and adoption support, free dinners at major campuses, on-site laundry and employee mall at Cupertino, relocation bonus, ESPP with 15% discount, and a generous 401k match. Source.











OpenAI Benefits

Extremely competitive cash, stock, and annual bonus packages, premium medical coverage, flexible working arrangements, and wellness resources supporting the fast pace of AI development. Source.











Google Benefits

Free meals and snacks, zero-premium health insurance in several locations, fertility and adoption support, extended parental leave, student-loan repayment match, and comprehensive on-site wellness resources. Source.











Facebook Benefits

Complimentary meals, on-site spa and wellness spaces, barbershop and clinic access, unlimited sick time, fertility and adoption allowances, a $2K baby bonus, full health coverage, and a 401k match up to federal limits. Source.











Netflix Benefits

Unlimited PTO, frequent free meals, premium medical, vision, and dental coverage, on-site fitness programs, strong disability insurance, and backup family and pet care resources. Source.
